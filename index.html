Bolstering the Clinical-Digital interface worldwide

Welcome to the 2023 Health CodeX Workshop, brought to you warmly by Nawab Group. Here, leading experts in Messaging and Coding of Disease Classification gather to share their insights on providing high quality healthcare through intuitive digital systems in the hands of clinicians.

Attendees can expect exposure to professionals in software development, standards for classification of diseases, diagnoses, therapies, procedures, digital integration of imaging, health records management, communication and exchange and many others. Health CodeX represents the epicentre of knowledge exchange on the complex, interdisciplinary endeavour that is the management and communication of healthcare data.

Witness Advancement

At Health CodeX, we invite experts in the fields of digital healthcare. Not only will representatives from standards and classifications organisations be attending, but we will also have the opportunity to see students and professionals present their own novel research work, potentially witnessing Advancement and impact happening before our own eyes!

Join us at UNSW!

This year, the University of New South Wales Kensington Campus is the proud host of the Health CodeX Workshop! With access to modern facilities and lecture theatres, the Kensington campus will see several workshop events and talks occurring concurrently thanks to its capacity. With restuarants and cafes nearby as well, UNSW makes an ideal venue.

The main hub of the workshop, The Roundhouse, in the lower portion of the campus, will be where the highest profile events and mingling will happen.
