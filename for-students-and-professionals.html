For Students & Professionals

As students and professionals, your research forms the basis of future innovation in the space of digital healthcare. Every year, the Health CodeX Workshop hosts a vast number of research papers and articles from a diverse range of people investigating the bettering of digitally delivered healthcare through one of its many branches. The sum of these contributions are what makes Health CodeX worth attending year after year, which is why we heavily value your attendance and warmly invite you to submit an abstract of your work.

For general guidelines on how to structure your abstract for submission, please see the following Paper published in the Indian Journal of Psychiatry. More specific guidelines and deadlines can be found below.

Writing Guidelines

  • The abstract in its entirety should not exceed 250 words.
  • There should be no subheadings within the abstract.
  • The following sections should be addressed within the abstract for completeness.
    • Background
    • Methods
    • Results
    • Conclusions
  • Each section should be line broken for clarity.
  • There should be no citations or references within the abstract.

Submission

Early submissions are encouraged.
The deadline for submission is 11:59pm 3rd November, 2023.
Please ensure your submission includes your abstract as selectable text for easy processing. The allowed file formats are .pdf, .txt, .docx, and .rtf.
